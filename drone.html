Quadcopter Drone Project

Overview:

A custom-built Quadcopter Drone designed for aerial surveillance and experimentation, controlled remotely using radio signals.

Key Components:

  • 🧠 KK2.1 Flight Controller
  • 📡 FlySky FS-i6 Transmitter & Receiver
  • 🔋 Li-Po Battery (11.1V)
  • ⚙️ 4 x BLDC Motors with ESCs
  • 🌀 Propellers (1045 or 1147)
  • 🧱 Lightweight Carbon Frame
  • 🔧 Power Distribution Board, Wires & Connectors

Functions:

  • Take-off & Landing via FS-i6 remote
  • Real-time manual navigation
  • Balanced control through KK2.1 LCD display
