RC Racing Car

Overview:

A remote-controlled racing car capable of high-speed performance with precise control using FS-i6 controller and Arduino logic.

Technologies Used:

  • Arduino Uno
  • FlySky FS-i6 Transmitter & Receiver
  • BLDC or Brushed Motor
  • Electronic Speed Controller (ESC)
  • Servo Motor for Steering
  • Li-Po Battery
  • Motor Driver, Chassis and Wheels

Features:

  • Forward/Reverse & Left/Right Control
  • High-Speed performance for racing events
  • Custom-built chassis for weight balance
